Beginner Full Body Workout

  1. Warm-Up Phase
    1. 5 minutes light cardio
    2. Dynamic stretching
      1. Arm circle: 30 seconds
      2. Leg swing: 30 seconds
      3. torse twists: 30 seconds
    3. Joint mobilization: 2 minutes
  2. Main Workout
    1. Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
    2. Push-ups: 3 sets to failures
    3. Bent-over Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
    4. Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  3. Cool-down
    1. Static Stretching: 5 minutees
    2. Deep Breathing: 2 minutes

Weekly Training Schedule Structure

Day 1: Upper Body Strngth

  • Bench Press: 4 seets x 8-12 reps
  • overhead Press: 3 sets x 10 -15 reps
  • Pull-ups : 3 sets to failure

Day 2: Lower Body Power

  • Barbell Squats: 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets x 5 reps
  • Lunges : 3 sets x 12 reps each leg

Day 3: Lower Body Power

  • Light cardio: 20-30 minutees
  • Mobility work
  • Foam rolling

Day 4: Lower Body Power

  • Kettebell Swings: 45 seconds
  • Push-ups: 45 seconds
  • Bodyweight Squats: 45 seconds
  • Rest: 60 seconds
  • Repeat 4 rounds
